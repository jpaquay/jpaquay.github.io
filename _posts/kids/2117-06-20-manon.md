### Brooklyn March

> I can’t read an urban legend  
on my phone, the sun’s so bright.  
Booted to the knee, a woman steps out  
and jaywalks the grid, swinging her hips,  
making busy taxis idle. Drivers baby  
potholes; letter carriers smile.
